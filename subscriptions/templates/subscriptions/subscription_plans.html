How Brick Heroes Works

At Brick Heroes, we believe in building, sharing, and having fun! Here’s how it works:

1. Choose Your Plan

Pick the subscription plan that suits your needs. Whether you're a casual builder or a dedicated enthusiast, we’ve got the right plan for you! Choose from the three tiers above, you will be directed to Stripe to pay for your recurring subscription amount. This will repeat monthly, but you can cancel anytime and you will not be charged again. If you want to upgrade or downgrade, simply cancel your subscription and when your active plan ends choose your new one!

2. Borrow Sets

Once you’ve subscribed, you can borrow LEGO sets based on your plan. Each set is carefully selected to provide hours of fun and creativity. Simply add the set/sets you want to borrow to your borrowing cart and complete the cart checkout process by providing delivery details, you order will be dispatched by us asap.

3. Return & Repeat

When you’re done building, simply return the set and borrow a new one. Mark the set as returned in your user profile and send back to us with the free postage packaging included when your set arrived. With our flexible system as soon as you mark a set as returned you are free to borrow new ones, you’re always just one return away from a new adventure!
